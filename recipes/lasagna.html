Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a dish made by intercalating cheese, sauce and the lasagna pasta.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Put sauce in your baking tray
  2. Put cheese over the sauce>
  3. Put lasagna pasta over the cheese
  4. Repeat until your tray is full
  5. Bake and serve
